How to Get an HDU Campus Card

14 September 2024 | Translations:
  • Zh-Cn

This is an English summary version of the original Chinese post.

Quick Overview#

If you do not have your HDU campus card yet, there are two practical options:

  1. Online reissue via the official WeChat service account (recommended).
  2. Offline reissue at the logistics service hall.

Recommended Online Flow#

  1. Find your student ID from the school service platform.
  2. Follow and register on the WeChat service account.
  3. Complete identity verification and account activation.
  4. Enter the campus card service and pay the card reissue fee.
  5. Wait for the pickup notification.

Offline Alternative#

You can also visit the logistics service hall and request a card reissue directly. Bring your ID and student credentials if possible.

Notes#

  • The original post includes detailed map screenshots and real photos.
  • It also includes WeChat/Alipay binding tips after card issuance.
